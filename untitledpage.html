What is Happycat studios?
Happycat Studios is an Indie game and Homemade animation studio based on indonesia
Our vision is to create a game that can be enjoyed by people from all walks of life, fostering connection and bringing joy to players everywhere. At the same time, we aspire to produce an independently crafted anime series that embodies our passion for storytelling and creativity. By combining these efforts, we hope to inspire others to pursue their dreams and show that with determination, teamwork, and a shared vision, anything is possible
